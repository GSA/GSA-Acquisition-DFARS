Subpart 205.4 - RELEASE OF INFORMATION

Parent topic: Part 205 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS

205.470 Contract clause.

Use the clause at 252.205-7000 , Provision of Information to Cooperative Agreement Holders, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, that are expected to exceed $1.5 million. This clause implements 10 U.S.C. 4957.
