244.101 Definitions.

As used in this subpart——

“Acceptable purchasing system” and “purchasing system” are defined in the clause at 252.244-7001 , Contractor Purchasing System Administration.

“Significant deficiency” is defined in the clause at 252.244-7001 , Contractor Purchasing System Administration.
