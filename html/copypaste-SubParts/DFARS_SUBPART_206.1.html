Subpart 206.1 - FULL AND OPEN COMPETITION

Parent topic: Part 206 - COMPETITION REQUIREMENTS

206.102 Use of competitive procedures.

(d) Other competitive procedures.

(2) In lieu of FAR 6.102(d)(2), competitive selection of science and technology proposals resulting from a broad agency announcement with peer or scientific review, as described in 235.016 (a) (10 U.S.C. 3012(2)).

206.102-70 Other competitive procedures.

Competitive selection of proposals based on a review by scientific, technological, or other subject-matter expert peers resulting from a commercial solutions opening as described in subpart 212.70 (10 U.S.C. 3458) is a competitive procedure.
