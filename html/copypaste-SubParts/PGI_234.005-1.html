PGI 234.005-1 Competition.

Upon request by Defense Pricing, Contracting, and Acquisition Policy (Contract Policy), contracting activities shall provide the benefits derived from the use of contract line items or contract options for the development and demonstration or initial production of technology developed under the contract, or the delivery of initial or additional items in accordance with DFARS 235.016 and 234.005-1.

Parent topic: PGI 234.005 General requirements.
