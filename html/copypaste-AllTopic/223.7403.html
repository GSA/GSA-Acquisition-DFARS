223.7403 Procedures.

After October 1, 2023, contracting officers shall only issue a solicitation to procure fire-fighting agent in accordance with performance specification MIL-PRF-24385F(SH), unless the requiring activity provides documentation of the exemption at 223.7402 Prohibition.. The contracting officer shall maintain the documentation in the contract file.
