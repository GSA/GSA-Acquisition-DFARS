225.7023-2 Restriction.

Except as provided in 225.7023-3, do not acquire a covered item from a covered country in accordance with section 802 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 (Pub. L. 117-81) (10 U.S.C. 4875).

Parent topic: 225.7023 Restriction on acquisition of personal protective equipment and certain other items from non-allied foreign nations.
