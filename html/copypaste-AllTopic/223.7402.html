223.7402 Prohibition.

After October 1, 2023, do not procure aqueous film-forming foam, which is used for fighting fires, that contains in excess of one part per billion perfluoroalkyl substances or polyfluoroalkyl substances. Procurements of fire-fighting agent for use solely onboard ocean-going vessels are exempt from this prohibition.
