PGI 225.871-4 Statutory waivers.

Forward any request for waiver under a cooperative project to the Deputy Secretary of Defense, through the Principal Director,Defense Pricing and Contracting. The waiver request shall include a draft Determination and Findings for signature by the Deputy Secretary of Defense establishing that the waiver is necessary to significantly further NATO standardization, rationalization, and interoperability.

Parent topic: PGI 225.871 North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) cooperative projects.
