225.7010-1 Restrictions.

In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 4864, unless manufactured in the United States, Australia, Canada, or the United Kingdom, do not acquire:

(a) The following components of naval vessels to the extent they are unique to marine applications:

(1)Gyrocompasses.

(2)Electronic navigation chart systems.

(3)Steering controls.

(4)Pumps.

(5)Propulsion and machinery control systems.

(6) Totally enclosed lifeboats.

(b) Large medium-speed diesel engines for new construction of auxiliary ships using funds available for National Defense Sealift Fund programs or Shipbuilding and Conversion, Navy.

Parent topic: 225.7010 Restrictions on certain naval vessel and auxiliary ship components.
