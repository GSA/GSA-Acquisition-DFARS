PGI 225.7021 Disclosure requirements for employment transparency regarding individuals who perform work in the People’s Republic of China.

Contracting officers shall provide disclosures received from a covered entity in response to the provision at DFARS 252.225-7057 and the clause at DFARS 252.225-7058 to Defense Pricing, Contracting, and Acquisition Policy, Contract Policy (DPCAP/CP) via email at osd.pentagon.ousd-a-s.mbx.dpc-cp@mail.mil.

  • PGI 225.7021-3 National security waiver of disclosure.
