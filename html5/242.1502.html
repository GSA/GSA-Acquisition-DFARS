This class deviation establishes thresholds for past performance reporting on DoD actions and requires past performance reporting for contracts awarded under FAR 8.6, Acquisition from Federal Prison Industries, Inc., and FAR subpart 8.7, Acquisition from Nonprofit Agencies Employing People Who are Blind or Severely Handicapped, when the thresholds in this class deviation are exceeded. This deviation is effective until incorporated in the DFARS or rescinded.
