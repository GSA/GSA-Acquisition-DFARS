PGI 210.070 Market research report guide for improving the tradecraft in services acquisition.

The “Market Research Report Guide for Improving the Tradecraft in Services Acquisition” is designed to effect the collection and sharing of standardized market research information across the Department. The guide is available for download at http://www.acq.osd.mil/dpap/cpic/cp/market_research.html.
